DitherLab

Mac-native image dither studio

Coming soon

DitherLab is built to make pixel-level control fast and fun. Drop an image into the app, choose a dither strategy, tune palette controls, and export exactly what you want.

Mac App Store (coming soon)

How it works

DitherLab uses classic and modern algorithms so every image can be shaped to a clear style. You can swap methods quickly and compare output to your original source on demand.

Core capabilities

  • 21+ dithering methods with adjustable strength and fine-grained options.
  • Image controls for contrast, saturation, gamma, exposure, vibrance, tone curve, and sharpening.
  • Manual palette workflows with many presets and swatch control.
  • Non-destructive editing with instant source-versus-output preview.
  • Export PNG, TIFF, GIF, or BMP with optional nearest-neighbor upscaling.
